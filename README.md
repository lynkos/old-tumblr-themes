## [Theme Dimensional](theme-dimensional.html)
### Description
- Choose highlight colour
- Choose of standard, rotating square links or pixel icons
- Extremely customisable pop-up links+ask
- One column or two column theme choice
- Customisable post background
- Customisable chat line colour
- Customisable scrollbar colour
- Customisable question background
- Customisable post shadows (shadows around your posts)
- Fade-in fade-out hover description
- Hover 3D effect title
- The choice to show your title or not
- The choice to have rounded corners or not
- The choice to have a stretched background or not
- The choice to have heart permalinks or to have fading strip-like permalinks
- Control over a corner image
- This theme holds a lot of links; at most nine (9) links, and at least four (4) links
- Diverse font choices - The font choices include Tinytots or Happy Monkey for body fonts, Cedarville Cursive or Permanent Marker for post title fonts, and Megrim or Codystar for blog title fonts
- ... And tons more!
